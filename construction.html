Founded by the League of American Bicyclists, National Bike Month is celebrated every May to promote bicycling for fun, fitness, and transportation. Bike Month Raleigh brings together a wide range of groups, including local nonprofits, local and regional government agencies, parks, bike shops, race teams, social clubs, breweries, coffee shops, and many more, to celebrate the fun and excitement of life on two wheels in Raleigh.

To learn more about National Bike Month, visit bikeleague.org.

To learn more about Oaks and Spokes, visit oaksandspokes.org or follow @oaksandspokes on Instagram or Facebook.
